# Mom postpartum timeline — research outline (working notes)

> Internal curation source for `src/data/momMilestones.js`. Not user-facing.

## Bibliography (high level)

| Org | Relevance |
|-----|-----------|
| ICMR-NIN | Maternal nutrition, iron, hydration, breastfeeding calories |
| IAP | Indian pediatric context; 6-week baby check often aligns with mom follow-up |
| WHO | Maternal mental health, postnatal care framework |
| NHS | Postnatal checks (~6 weeks), bleeding norms, mood screening |
| ACOG | Postpartum visit timing, exercise return, mood disorders |

## Weeks 0–2 (fourth trimester)

- Rest, sleep when baby sleeps, accept help
- Lochia / bleeding: what's typical vs call doctor
- Perineal or C-section incision basics → link `cSection`, `recovery`
- Feeding establishment → `nippleCare`, `nutrition`
- Mood: baby blues vs PPD screen → `mentalHealth`
- Pelvic rest, no heavy lifting

## Weeks 3–6

- 6-week (42-day) postpartum visit — India often 6 weeks; book ahead
- Gentle walking → `walking`
- Pelvic floor awareness → `pelvicFloor`
- Posture while feeding → `posture`
- Sleep fragmentation normal → `sleep`
- Contraception / spacing conversation with provider

## Weeks 7–12

- Clearance for gradual exercise (provider-dependent)
- Return-to-work planning if applicable
- Hair shedding normal → `hairLoss`
- Intimacy / comfort — when cleared by doctor
- Baby blues should improve; persistent low mood → `mentalHealth`

## Months 3–4

- Energy may still lag; iron check if tired
- Diastasis / core — gentle progress → `exercise`, `pelvicFloor`
- Stretch marks fading → `stretchMarks`
- Hydration + balanced meals → `hydration`, `nutrition`

## Months 5–6

- Bone density borrowing during lactation → `boneDensity`
- Supplement review → `supplements`
- Massage / self-care → `massage`
- Weaning prep if relevant (optional bullet)

## Months 7–9

- Sustained mental health check-ins
- Exercise routine habit
- Work–life boundaries

## Months 10–12

- First birthday season fatigue
- Extended breastfeeding or weaning — personal choice
- Long-term pelvic floor maintenance
- Celebrate recovery progress

## Red flags (watchFor, all periods as needed)

- Soaking pad in an hour, large clots after week 2
- Fever, foul-smelling discharge
- Severe headache, vision changes, BP concerns
- Chest pain, leg swelling (DVT)
- Unable to care for self or baby; intrusive harm thoughts
